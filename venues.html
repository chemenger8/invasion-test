Our Venues

Overture Center for the Arts
201 State St.

The Overture Center for the Arts is a beautiful location seated in the heart of downtown Madison, one short block from the Capitol. All of the Saturday and Sunday classes will be located in this venue, as well as the Saturday dance. Pay close attention to the listed rooms on the schedule, as they will change by day.

Nearby Parking

Downtown Madison has plenty of reasonably priced parking options. Ramps will likely be your best option Saturday classes. Street parking is free after 6pm and on Sundays. Leave yourself extra time to travel to the Saturday dance. The Overture Center has evening events that may affect parking availability and traffic.

NEAR THE OVERTURE CENTER

Overture Center Parking Ramp
318 W. Mifflin St.

Entrance Locations: 300 W. Dayton St. & 300 W. Mifflin St.

State Street Capitol Parking Ramp
214 N. Carroll St.

Entrance Location: 200 N. Carroll St.
